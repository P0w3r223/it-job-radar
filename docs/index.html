it-job-radar

Polish IT job market — technology trends & salary ranges

What this is. A data pipeline that collects a sample of Polish IT job offers from theprotocol.it, normalizes them (unifying technology names, seniority levels and currencies), stores them in SQLite, and analyses which technologies are in demand and what they pay. Snapshot: 314 offers. B2B (net) and employment (gross) salaries are kept separate.

Methodology & limitations. A bounded, spread sample (not the whole base), respecting robots.txt and dropping personal data. Salaries are medians within one currency/contract kind; small snapshots and unit edge-cases (hourly rates) can skew rare buckets. See the repo's docs/research/data-sources.md.
